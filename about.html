Our Story

"Welcome to Sweet Angeles Bakery, where the flavors of Venezuela meet the heart of Bradford, ON! As a passionate baker and entrepreneur originally from Venezuela, I bring the authentic taste of homemade Venezuelan desserts and cakes to our community. With a background in running a successful food business back home, I've poured my love for baking into every treat I create. Whether you're craving traditional Venezuelan sweets or a beautifully crafted custom cake for your special occasion, we've got you covered. Join us on a delightful journey of flavors and indulge in the irresistible creations that make every moment sweeter.

Venezuelan

So Good

Venezuelan bakeries blend rich culinary traditions with vibrant flavors. From the streets of Caracas to cozy corners, they offer a tempting array of treats capturing the essence of Venezuelan cuisine. Imagine warm arepas with savory fillings, fluffy empanadas, and sweet pastries like golfeados or bienmesabe. Each bite tells a story of heritage and passion, celebrating diverse ingredients and techniques. Whether it's a quick bite or a leisurely indulgence, Venezuelan bakeries invite you to savor tradition and experience Latin American warmth.
